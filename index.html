01 — about

My Story

My journey wasn't a straight line. I spent three years at Hassan II University (FSAC Ain Chock) in physics and chemistry sciences, then enrolled in an electromechanical automation program at ISTA. Those paths taught me what I didn't want.

But back in May 2021, something already told me where I belonged — I completed the Full Stack Development Track through Udacity, as part of the UAE's 1 Million Arab Coders Initiative. That certification was the spark.

After years of exploring the wrong directions, I came back to what I knew was right. I'm now in my first year of Web Development at ISTA — not by accident, but by choice. My goal is to master software engineering and eventually move into AI, to build things that solve real problems in the world.

This time, I'm not lost. I know exactly where I'm going.
